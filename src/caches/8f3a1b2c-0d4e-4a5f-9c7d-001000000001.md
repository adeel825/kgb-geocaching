## Mission Briefing

Welcome to the Khan Geocaching Bureau, Agent. You have located the inaugural drop
of our field operations programme. This cache was placed under the supervision of
Agent Hamza following intelligence received from a courier known only as Agent
Squirrel.

## Your Orders, Agent

Congratulations on a successful recovery. You are now part of a small and discreet
network. Sign the logbook inside the container with your codename and today's
date. If you carried a token of trade, you may exchange it for the one within.
When finished, return the cache to its precise hiding place. **Do not
re-hide it elsewhere.** Other agents must be able to find it.
