# {{ title }}

## Mission Briefing

**CLASSIFIED — EYES ONLY — JUNIOR OPERATIVE CLEARANCE REQUIRED**

Agent, the Bureau has received intelligence that a KGB Mole has concealed a packet of secret documents somewhere within this very shopping mall. The Mole blends in effortlessly — possibly browsing a window display, possibly debating whether the cinnamon pretzel is worth it. Your assignment: locate the hidden cache before the Mole finishes their shopping. Field Agent Asad has planted the cache personally and is now observing from a comfortable position — almost certainly the food court, with a beverage.

## Your Orders, Agent

Well done — you have located the cache, and the Bureau is deeply impressed. Please do the following:

1. Sign the logbook with your agent name and today's date. The Bureau keeps meticulous records. Meticulous.
2. If the cache contains a trade item and you wish to take it, leave something of equal or greater value. This is non-negotiable Bureau policy.
3. Return the cache precisely as you found it. The Mole must not suspect a thing.
4. Log your find online so Agent Asad can confirm the mission from wherever he is sitting (almost certainly still the food court).

**Cover Story:** Should a curious shopper or passing security guard enquire about your activities, you are simply searching for a lost balloon. Alternatively, you are comparing the sole thickness of two pairs of trainers. Either explanation is fully Bureau-approved.

## Field Notes

The KGB has long considered shopping malls ideal operational environments: crowded, loud, and full of people staring at their phones rather than at junior operatives on covert missions. Agent Asad chose this location with great care and only mild deliberation. Should you spot someone at the food court looking suspiciously relaxed, do give them a small nod. They will know what it means.

## Sign-Off

*Congratulations, Agent. You are hereby certified as an official KGB Mall Operative — Class II. Agent Asad is proud, and the Bureau is pleased. Now go find the pretzel stand. You have earned it.*

— The Khan Geocaching Bureau
