## Mission Briefing

Agent, your file has been reviewed and your application to the Khan Geocaching Bureau has been approved — effective immediately. Your first assignment brings you to a well-known field station used by KGB operatives for generations: an outdoor communal gathering area, favoured for its cover as a perfectly ordinary family destination. Handler Asad has secured a covert drop here, and it is your job to retrieve it.

## Your Orders, Agent

Upon arrival at the rendezvous point, do not rush. A seasoned operative surveys the area first. Take note of your surroundings — count the number of communal cooking stations you can see, then look for something nearby that seems just a little too tidy, a little too deliberately placed. That is no accident. That is KGB craftsmanship.

Once you have located the container, handle it with the professionalism befitting your new rank. Inside you will find the Mission Ledger. You are required to confirm your operative identity by signing your name (or your chosen codename — we recommend something excellent). Return everything precisely as you found it. A good agent leaves no trace.

Log your find online as well, so Bureau Headquarters can update your service record.

## Field Notes

Handler Asad has been running field stations like this one since the Bureau's early days. He has a talent for choosing spots that feel ordinary to the untrained eye. You, however, are no longer untrained. Welcome to the KGB — the family business.
