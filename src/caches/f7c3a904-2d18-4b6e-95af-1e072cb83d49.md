# {{ title }}

## Mission Briefing

Congratulations, Agent. You have just located Cache #001 — the very first drop ever placed by the Khan Geocaching Bureau. On this very trail in Central Jersey, the founding members of the KGB gathered and made a solemn (and very official) decision: the world needed a geocaching agency, and the Khan family was going to build it. With a handshake, a great deal of enthusiasm, and exactly one container, the Bureau was born.

## Your Orders, Agent

You found it — well done. Your first act as an official KGB field agent is to sign the log inside. Print your name (or your agent codename, which is strongly encouraged). Then seal the container, return it precisely as you discovered it, and leave no trace that the Bureau was ever here. Future agents are counting on you. Once you are home, log your find online and record your field report — even the smallest details can be useful to Bureau headquarters.

## Field Notes

The founding members stood right where you are standing now and looked out at this trail with one thought: *this is where it begins.* KGB-001 is not merely a cache — it is a declaration. The Bureau's mission is to scatter adventure across every corner of the map, one hidden container at a time. Dozens more drops are already in the works. More missions. More trails. More mysteries. This is only Page One.

## Sign-Off

You are now part of the Bureau's permanent record, Agent. The KGB thanks you for your service.
